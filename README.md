# Privacy Policy — DropTally
DropTally is a personal delivery-drop and earnings tracker that runs entirely on your device.

What we collect: Nothing. DropTally has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no servers. It does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

Your data: Every drop, rate, and setting you enter is stored only on your device. We never see it. You can export a backup or clear everything from inside the app at any time, and uninstalling removes all of its data.

Permissions: Display over other apps (the optional floating bubble), Notifications (to keep the bubble running), and Vibrate (tap feedback). None of them send data anywhere.
